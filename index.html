A little about Rolando

Originally from Mexico, Rolando is a Web Developer, UX/UI Designer, and a Nashville Software School Graduate. Previously, he received a Bachelors Degree of Science in Information Technology.

His work history has provided him a variety of technical and business skills which allow him to relate to all levels of management, to a variety of business positions, and work successfully with a diversity of personalities, customers, and end-users.

He is a problem solver, and have always found satisfaction in discovering and implementing solutions.

He spends his days as a UX/UI designer, illustrator and front-end developer at Propel Wellness®. Using everything from sketches to graphic's programs to code, he creates logos, icons, web designs, and fully fleshed out websites.

His energy source is his passion for creating engaging applications and intuitive interfaces that bridge the gap between functionality and design.

In his free time, he loves spending time with his family: his wife, Emily, and his two children, Violet and Levi. He can also be found playing golf, or screaming his head off at a Titans football game, a Preds hockey game, and a Nashville SC soccer match.
